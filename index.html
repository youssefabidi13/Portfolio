2023

This web application enables electricity supplier's customers to enter and access their bills using HTML, CSS, JavaScript, PHP, Bootstrap, and MySQL. Customers can input their electricity consumption at the end of each month and upload a photo of the meter. They can also view their electricity bills and submit complaints, which the admin can respond to. Suppliers can manage customers by adding or editing their information. At the end of each year, they can validate customer-entered electricity consumption details and generate bills containing customer information, consumption, and price (HT and TTC).
